Sago Street

Sago St, Singapore 059014

Daily: 24 Hours

Coordinates: 1.2818094268650042, 103.8440098125536

While this area may have gotten its name from the sago factories located here during the 1840s,
it was more famous for the Chinese death houses that were along the neighbouring Sago Lane*.
As most early immigrants were here without their family, the dying would come and live
out their last days on the upper floors of the death house – while funerals were held right below,
on the ground floor. Though it was known as “street of the dead”, it was anything but quiet.
