Malay Heritage Centre

Opened in June 2005 by Lee Hsien Loong, this former istana of kampong gelam has become a location that provides wonderful cultural exposure and learing opportunities for visitors of all ages and interests. Through the exhibits, programmes and activities, the centre hopes to honour the past while providing a means for present-day expression.
